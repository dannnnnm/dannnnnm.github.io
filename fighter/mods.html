Fighter Mutations

bouncingBetty : Projectiles bounce off walls

risingTension : Projectiles deal more damage with each bounce

repelPhys : As a player nears death, there's a chance a projectile will bounce off of him

meleeOnly : Exactly what it says on the tin

staticBetty : Projectiles that move in only one direction will always remain that way

doomMove : Diagonal movement is faster than single-direction movement

fairGame : Players will always have the same amount of health
